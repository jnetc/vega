Features that Increase Efficiency Timely Workflow

Eiffels AI transcends traditional apps with its swift, no-install integration directly into WhatsApp, streamlining construction management. This transformative AI solution delves deep into industry workflows, simplifying and automating processes without the complexities of typical software setups.

AI Daily Reports

  • Automatically generate comprehensive daily reports using voice, image or text inputs. Eiffels AI captures essential data, organizes it, and prepares polished reports ready for submission and approval.
  • Log activities and materials on-the-go.
  • Send reports directly to managers for fast approval from WhatsApp to WhatsApp.

Blockchain Secured Approvals

  • Ensure the integrity of your documents with blockchain technology. Every approval is traceable and tamper-proof, providing peace of mind and reliability.
  • Secure your data with blockchain's immutable ledger.
  • Speed up the approval process without compromising security.

AI-Powered Translations

  • Communicate effectively in multiple languages within your diverse team. Get real-time, accurate translations to keep everyone on the same page.
  • Translate documents and messages instantly using your voice.
  • Facilitate smoother communication across global teams.

Smart Invoicing

  • Generate and track invoices directly through WhatsApp with UKKO.fi integration. Reduce manual entry and focus more on your project tasks.
  • Streamline payment processes with automated solutions.
  • Keep track of payments and financial status effortlessly.

/ ABOUT VEGA BI

Vega is a digital innovation company focused on transforming how businesses interact with their data. We build real-time SaaS solutions that help companies track performance, manage operations, and make smarter decisions — all through intuitive web interfaces and seamless mobile communication.

Our platform integrates web dashboards, WhatsApp automation, and advanced analytics into a single ecosystem designed for everyday business use. Unlike traditional tools, Vega offers a user-first experience — no complex integrations, no technical barriers — just clear insights, instant reporting, and real-world flexibility.

We believe that data should empower, not overwhelm. That's why we design systems that adapt to the business — not the other way around. Our mission is simple: help companies grow by making information actionable, processes faster, and communication smarter.
