Eiffels AI transcends traditional apps with its swift, no-install integration directly into WhatsApp, streamlining construction management. This transformative AI solution delves deep into industry workflows, simplifying and automating processes without the complexities of typical software setups.

AI Daily Reports

  • How do I get started with Eiffels AI?

    To get started, simply sign up on our website or directly through WhatsApp scanning the QR code. Once registered, you can immediately begin using the features available in your chosen package.

  • Is there a free trial available for Eiffels AI?

    Yes, we offer a 30-day FREE trial for new users! No payment details required! This allows you to explore all the features of Eiffels AI and see how it can benefit your construction management processes before making a financial commitment.

  • How does Eiffels AI ensure the security of my data?

    Eiffels AI uses blockchain technology to secure approvals and transactions, ensuring that all your data is immutable, traceable, and protected from tampering. Additionally, Meta's WhatsApp is entirely encrypted, and all data transmitted via WhatsApp cannot be accessed by Eiffels AI or Meta. It is 100% secure and encrypted, adhering to the highest standards of data protection globally.
